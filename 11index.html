About US

About PETOPIA

PETOPIA was set up to provide dog owners with an alternative to leaving their dogs 'home alone' while they work or go on vacation - - a place where all the staff are fully trained and equipped with all the knowledge and skills needed to provide a safe, caring and fun environment for dogs.

Responsible dog owners want the best for their dogs – good care in a safe environment that can provide them with companionship and exercise. Dogs need stimulation to minimize issues such as separation anxiety and boredom which can result in constant barking and destruction. Most people cannot take their dog to work or to other places where dogs are not allowed, but want to have peace of mind in knowing that their dogs are well taken care of until they can take care of them again.
